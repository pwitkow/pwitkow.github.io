About Me

I'm a Graduate Student researcher working with Joy Geng and Erie Boorman at the Center for Mind and Brain at UC Davis.

I study the neural mechanisms of learning and attention by using neuroimaging, machine-learning, and computational models to understand how people learn the sturcture of their environment and allocate attention based on that knowledge.
